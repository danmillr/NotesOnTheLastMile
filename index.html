Notes on the Last Mile

About RHAFTS Last-Mile Coalition:

There are several elected officials and organizations working toward solutions to the impacts Last-Mile e-commerce distribution centers bring to vulnerable neighborhoods in NYC like ours. Our community is struggling to understand how 6 distribution centers can practically and safely operate in our tiny neighborhood with limited truck routes. Our aim is to share information about these possible solutions in a transparent and open forum.
